‘What kind of tree is that?’ ‒I ask myself. ‘And those flowers?’ In that moment I understand they are there as a protection, to support the community of people in the studio and myself.

Together we finish the workshops. Marcela Armas suggests we use a left over tin of paint, and she covers with it the steel beams on the ground floor. When I come down I get surprised: the beams are painted in red! Now the space is red and black; red is a live color, and the idea is good, but a bit intense for me. We leave it for now, but in the long term we should reconsider the color.
